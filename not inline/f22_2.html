Get Ready, Our Recruiting Cycle is About to Kick Off!

We're so glad you're interested in MEG and we are so excited to meet you! Below we have listed all our remaining recruiting events. These events are a great opportunity to learn more about MEG and ask questions.

Mass Meetings are a fantastic way to get to know what MEG is all about. Come and see what we do, how our projects work, our professional development programs, and much more. Also take the opportunity to meet the members and network!

Want to talk to our members one on one? Come to our Coffee Chat to network and ask questions! Learn about what our members have done and see where you can fit at MEG. Coffee Chats are a great place to learn more about everything from everyday project work to leadership opportunities

At the second of our two Mass Meetings, we will be covering the same things as the first. Feel free to come to either to learn about the Club in general, and also meet the members!

Casing is an essential part of consulting and club recruiting, but we understand that most of our applicants have very little experience casing. No need to worry, MEG provides a Case and Resume Workshop to walk through the basics of casing and also the fundamentals of resume writing. We'll walk you step by step through a case and help you get acquainted with casing as a whole.

At MEG we understand that diversity equity and inclusion is important for making everyone we work with, from out members to our clients to our applicants, feel respected and prepared to succeed. Our DEI Event is a fantastic way to learn about MEG member's experiences when it comes to DEI within both our club, the University, and the business world.

If you have any questions free feel to email us at meg.eboard@umich.edu
